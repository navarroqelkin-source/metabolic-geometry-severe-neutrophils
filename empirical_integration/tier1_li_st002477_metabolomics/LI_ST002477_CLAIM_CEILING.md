# Li ST002477 — Claim Ceiling

Evidence tier: **Tier 1** (real processed quantitative metabolomics matrix with provenance).

## Permitted
- Metabolic **state / constraint** of human neutrophils.
- Descriptive comparison across COVID-19 severity (Control / Mild / Severe).
- Relative direction (higher/lower median) of a named metabolite between groups.
- Exploratory descriptive ranking of group differences.
- Mapping metabolites to pan-omic modules as **supporting** tiered evidence.

## Prohibited
- Metabolic **flux** or pathway activity **rate** (a static pool size is not a flux).
- **Causal** mechanism or directionality of causation.
- **Clinical prediction**, diagnosis, prognosis, or validated biomarker claims.
- **Executed neutrophil function** (NETosis rate, ROS burst, killing, migration, degranulation).
- **Sample-level fusion** with unpaired sources (NeuMap, Sadiku, Chapman, PXD029046, GSE141285).
- Pan-omic latent integration (MOFA / WNN / MultiVI) across unpaired layers.

## Interpretation rule
Metabolomics measures the **state** of the metabolite pool, not the rate of its turnover.
Severity contrasts are **descriptive associations**, not clinical or causal claims.
