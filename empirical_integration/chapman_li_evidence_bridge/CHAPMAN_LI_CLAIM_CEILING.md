# Chapman–Li Bridge — Claim Ceiling

## Permitted synthesis
- Chapman defines NET/release proteome **material composition** (histones, MPO, neutrophil elastase,
  citrullinated peptides).
- Li_ST002477 defines a severity-associated metabolomic **state** (M05 glycolysis/PPP supported).
- Together they support an evidence-tiered link between **metabolic state** and **neutrophil
  extracellular effector material** as complementary, non-equivalent layers of neutrophil biology.

## Prohibited
- Sample-level fusion / correlation between Chapman and Li (no shared samples; unpaired).
- Co-measurement claim.
- Causal inference; metabolism→NETosis mechanism; "metabolic severity causes NET formation".
- NETosis **rate**, NET **clearance**, or NET **pathogenicity** from either dataset.
- "Li demonstrates NETosis"; "Chapman measures NETosis rate"; metabolic flux; biomarker; function.

## Tiers
- Chapman_PXD011796: Tier 3 (article/NET proteome; repository raw-only → Tier 2 only if the
  open-access supplement table is parsed).
- Li_ST002477: Tier 1 (real matrix + provenance + QC + kernel-supported separation + localization).
- Bridge: evidence-tiered material/module/concept synthesis only.
